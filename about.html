Our Mission

To empower communities economically through sustainable recycling initiatives, with a special focus on women and youth, while creating innovative solutions for waste management and environmental preservation.

Our Vision

A sustainable, equitable future where communities drive local innovation, women lead economic transformation, and every street is free from plastic waste through community-driven recycling initiatives.

Our Unique Value Proposition

A world where waste becomes a resource, communities thrive through sustainable practices, and environmental consciousness drives every action.

Our Organizational Model

Gjenge For Earth PRO (GPRO)

Non-profit organization focused on scaling Material Recovery Facilities and building sustainable waste management systems.

  • Community empowerment and job creation
  • Supporting local recyclers and waste aggregators
  • Environmental impact through plastic waste recovery
  • Grant management and donor relations
Gjenge Makers Ltd Partnership

Strategic partnership providing technical support and ensuring long-term sustainability.

  • Machinery and technical support provision
  • Initial funding for facility setup and operations
  • Guaranteed plastic sourcing at subsidized rates
  • Research & development for machinery efficiency
How Our Partnership Model Works
Initial Setup

Gjenge Makers provides machinery and operational funding to establish MRFs

Material Exchange

GPRO provides recycled plastic to Gjenge Makers at subsidized rates for production

Debt Settlement

Setup costs are compensated through plastic equivalent value over time
