A PLANET-SIZED PROBLEM

Kenya generates over 24 million tons of waste annually, with plastic waste making up a significant portion. Most of this waste ends up in landfills, waterways, and the environment, causing devastating effects on ecosystems and communities.

Plastic pollution is a crisis requiring urgent action. Without intervention, this waste persists for decades, harming wildlife and human health while communities lack sustainable building materials and economic opportunities.

WE AIM TO PROCESS 100,000 TONS OF PLASTIC WASTE BY 2030
